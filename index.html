DELTA State

Delta State sits in the South-South region of Nigeria and is often called "The Big Heart" for its role in the country's oil production and agriculture. Its capital, Asaba, lies along the western bank of the River Niger, while Warri, my hometown, serves as the state's commercial center and a hub for the maritime and oil industries. The state was created in 1991 when the old Bendel State was split into Delta and Edo, and today it is made up of 25 local government areas under Governor Sheriff Oborevwori.
